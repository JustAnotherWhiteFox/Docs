# Study Tecnics

Pomodoro
-----------
* Study for 25min 
* rest for 5min 
* repead 4 times, and rest for 15min

Lozanov Tecnic 
----------------
* Study with background music
* voiceless musics
* e.g classic music or lo-fi (60-70 bpm)

Feymann's Rule 
---------------
* study 
* explain to a child
* indentify failures
* simplify, refine and study more
